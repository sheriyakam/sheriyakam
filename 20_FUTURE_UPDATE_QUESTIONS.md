# 🚀 20 Future Update Questions & Ideas for Sheriyakam

*This document is solely for noting down advanced future updates, features, and out-of-the-box ideas to consider as Sheriyakam scales into a massive platform.*

---

### 📱 Technical App Features
1. **Offline Mode:** How can partners view their next scheduled job, customer details, and basic map directions even if their mobile network drops in a rural area?
2. **AI Diagnostic Camera:** Will customers eventually be able to upload a photo of a broken wire/appliance, and have an AI instantly estimate the required parts and labor cost before a worker arrives?
3. **Multi-Language Voice Search:** Will older customers be able to open the app, tap a microphone, and speak in Malayalam ("Fan kanangunnilla" / Fan is not spinning) to have the app auto-fill the booking?
4. **AR (Augmented Reality) Remote Guidance:** Can we add an emergency video call feature where the technician can view the customer's fuse box remotely and draw circles on the screen to show exactly which breaker to turn off for safety before the tech arrives?
5. **Gamification for Workers:** Should there be a "Leaderboard" in the partner app showing who completed the most jobs with 5-star ratings this week, automatically rewarding the winner with a ₹1,000 cash bonus?
6. **In-App Wallet Auto-Topup:** For workers paying a commission, can we link their bank accounts via UPI auto-pay to automatically top up their "Sheriyakam Wallet" when it hits ₹0, ensuring they never miss job notifications?
7. **Smart Home Integration (IoT):** Could Sheriyakam partner with smart home brands so that if a smart device fails, it automatically sends a repair alert to the Sheriyakam app?

### 💼 Business & Monetization Scaling
8. **Hardware Sales Store (B2B):** Should Sheriyakam build an internal wholesale e-commerce store where approved partners can buy wires, switches, and tools at a 15% discount directly inside the app?
9. **"Sheriyakam Prime" Subscription:** Will you introduce a model where customers pay ₹999/year for zero visiting fees, flat 10% off all labor, and priority assigning during peak monsoon emergencies?
10. **Franchising the Tech (White-labeling):** If Sheriyakam succeeds in Malabar, will you license the software to an agency in Kochi so they handle the physical workers, while you sit back and take a 5% software royalty?
11. **B2B AMC Dashboard:** Can we build a separate external web portal for Hotel and Hospital Managers to track all their Annual Maintenance Contracts and view monthly digital repair expenditure graphs?
12. **Dynamic Fleet Heatmaps:** Can the admin panel eventually feature a live "Demand Heatmap" showing where the most bookings are requested, so the admin can send push notifications telling idle workers to drive towards those "Hot Zones"?
13. **Time-Block Bidding (Surge):** If no workers are available immediately, can the app let the customer broadcast the job saying "I will pay a ₹200 bonus to whoever can come within 10 minutes!"?

### 🛠️ Logistics & Quality Control
14. **Automated Quality Control (Photo Proof):** Will the app eventually require the worker to take a verified "Before" and "After" photo of the electrical panel they fixed, using the camera in real-time, to ensure they aren't taking shortcuts?
15. **Instant Background Checks (API):** In the future, can we integrate directly with government/police API databases to automatically verify a new worker's criminal record or Aadhaar identity instantly upon signup?
16. **Dynamic Spare Parts Inventory tracking:** Can a worker log in the app that they used a specific "10 Amp Switch", and have the app remember their digital inventory, automatically reminding them to restock it next week?
17. **"Bring a Helper" Feature:** For heavy jobs like AC installation, can the app allow a main technician to temporarily "Link" a junior helper's profile to the job via QR code, so the system automatically splits the bank payout 70/30?

### 👥 Customer Experience Magic
18. **Digital Warranty Cards:** Instead of paper receipts, will the app automatically generate a digital 30-day "Sheriyakam Guarantee Certificate" PDF that the customer can download and store?
19. **Split Material Payments:** Can customers have the option to pay 50% upfront via UPI specifically to fund the required materials the worker needs to buy, and the remaining 50% labor charge post-completion?
20. **NRI Family Account Linking:** Can an NRI working in Dubai securely link their app account to their parents' house in Kerala, allowing them to book, track the technician live on a map, and pay for the electrician remotely?
