# Step 3: Enable Google Sign-In

## Instructions:

1. In the left sidebar, click on **"Build"** to expand it
2. Click on **"Authentication"**
3. Click the **"Get started"** button (if you see it)
4. Click on the **"Sign-in method"** tab at the top
5. Find **"Google"** in the list of providers
6. Click on **"Google"**
7. Toggle the **"Enable"** switch to ON
8. Select a **"Project support email"** from the dropdown (use your email)
9. Click **"Save"**

## What you should see:
- Google provider should now show as "Enabled" in the list
- Status should change from disabled to enabled

## Screenshot this step when done!

Once you've enabled Google, take a screenshot and we'll move to step 4.
